Thelonius Monk

 

Biography and Information

  • Born in North Carolina on October 10, 1917.
  • Learned classical piano early in life and had an early aptitude for it.
  • Dropped out of school at age 17 and assembled his own quartet.
  • Began collaborating with Jazz musician Dizzie Gillespie in 1941.
  • Created his first recording in 1944. From 1947 to 1952, he created a few recordings that defined his early original/innovative style.
  • Critics regarded his first "masterpiece album" as the 1957 release, Brilliant Corners.
  • Second-half of career held with high acclaim. Given a contract by Columbia Records in 1962.
  • Passed away from a stroke in 1982, leaving behind an influential legacy that inspired many.

"A genius is one who is most like himself." - Thelonius Monk
